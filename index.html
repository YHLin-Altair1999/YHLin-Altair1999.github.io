Research

The immense energy output from active galactic nuclei (AGN) are believed to have a great impact on galaxy evolution across cosmic time. Specifically, relativistic jets greatly alters the evolution of galaxy clusters in the universe by heating the intracluster medium (ICM) and preventing the runaway cooling of cool core (CC) clusters via self-regulated feedback cycles. The detailed interaction between AGN jets and the ICM is likely dependent on the energy content of AGN jet-inflated bubbles; however, the composition of AGN jets and bubbles remains poorly constrained observationally.

My research aims to investigate the evolution of AGN bubbles with different energy composition. Specifically, I foucs on cosmic-ray proton (CRp) dominated and cosmic-ray electron (CRe) dominated bubbles. I am interested in the energies, dynamics, heating and other radio/x-ray observables.

Science Communication

Since 2017, my friends an I established EASY Astronomy and Earth Science promotion team, aiming to share the beauty of astronomy and Earth science with general public by writing articles, makeing videos and giving talks.
Besides our own FB, IG and Blogger, we are also collaberating with Science Monthly (the oldest science magazine in Taiwan) and Pansci (the largeset online pop-science platform in Taiwan).
As the chief editor, my work is to manage the schedule of posting, reviewing my teammates' article and contact with other editors. From time to time, I would also come up with new idea and lead my team to achieve the goal.

Community

I have been heavily involved in the amature astronmoy community in Taiwan.

In 2017, I joined NTHU astronomy club and later served as the staff of Astro-Camp (2018), education officer (2018 - 2019), vice director (2019 - 2020) and the organizer of astrophysics study group (AstroRead, 2018 - 2020).
During those years, I, along with other club cabinets, have host tens of club lecture, seminar, observation campaign and other activities.

From 2018, I start to get involved in ACUUT, the union of astronomy club of the universities in Taiwan. I served as the education officer in the 5th Astro-Festival (2018), the coordinator of the Camp of Leadership (2021), supervisor (2021 - Current) the coordinator of the workshop in astrophotography (2022) and the chief editor of the science communication group (2022 - Current).
From 2019, I have served as the supervisor of HGHS astronomy club.
